Type: calculation

Difficulty: 1/10

Text of a problem: A body of mass m=2 kg moves on a smooth horizontal surface under the action of a force \( \pmb{F=6 N} \). Find the acceleration of the body.

Answer: \( \pmb{a = 3 \text { } m/s^{2}} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{(1) F = ma} \)
\( \pmb{(2) a = { F \over m } } \)
