Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 2/10

Text of a problem: The work function of a material is 2 electron-volts. The energy of the incident photon is 3 electron-volts. Find the maximum kinetic energy of the emitted photoelectron.

Answer:
\( \pmb{E_{max} = 1 eV} \)

Solution:
\(\text{(1)} \pmb{E = A + E_{max}} \)
\(\text{(2)} \pmb{E_{max} = E - A} \)
