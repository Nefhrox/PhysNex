Electricity and Magnetism Formulae

Electrostatics

\( \pmb{F = k \frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2}} \) - Coulomb's law

\( \pmb{E = \frac{F}{q}} \) - Electric field strength

\( \pmb{V = k \frac{q}{r}} \) - Electric potential

\( \pmb{C = \frac{q}{V}} \) - Capacitance

Current Electricity

\( \pmb{I = \frac{q}{t}} \) - Electric current

\( \pmb{V = IR} \) - Ohm's law

\( \pmb{P = IV} \) - Electric power

Magnetism

\( \pmb{F = qvB \sin(\theta)} \) - Magnetic force on a moving charge

\( \pmb{B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}} \) - Magnetic field due to a long straight conductor
