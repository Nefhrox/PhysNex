Text of a problem: A long straight wire carries a current of 5 A. Find the magnetic field at a distance of 0.2 m from the wire

Answer: \( \pmb{B = 5 \times 10^{-6} \text { } T} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{B = {μ₀I \over 2πr}} \)
