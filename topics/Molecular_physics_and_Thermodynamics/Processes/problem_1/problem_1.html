Text of a problem: \( \pmb{ \text{Carnot cycle } T_H = 600 \text{K, } T_L = 300 \text{K. Calculate efficiency } \eta.} \)

Answer: \( \pmb{\eta = 0.5} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{\eta = 1 - \frac{T_L}{T_H}} \)
