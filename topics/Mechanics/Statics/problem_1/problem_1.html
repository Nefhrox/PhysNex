Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 3/10

Text of a problem: Lever arms 0.2 m and 0.8 m. Force \( \pmb{F_1 = 100 N} \) on short arm. Calculate \( \pmb{F_2} \) on long arm for equilibrium.

Answer: \( \pmb{F_2 = 25 N} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{(1) \text { } F_1 l_1 = F_2 l_2} \)
\( \pmb{(2) \text { } F_2 = {F_1 l_1 \over l_2}} \)
