Type: calculation

Difficulty: 2/10

Text of a problem: The initial amount is \( \pmb{N_0 = 8 \cdot 10^{10} \text{ the final amount }} N = 2 \cdot 10^{10} \text{,}\)the half-life is T=122s. Find the time t.

Answer: \( \pmb{t = 244s} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{N = N_0 \cdot 2^{-\frac{t}{T}} \text{ |:} N} \)
\( \pmb{1 = \frac{N_0}{N} \cdot 2^{-\frac{t}{T}}} \)
\( \pmb{\frac{1}{4} = 2^{-\frac{t}{T}}} \)
\( \pmb{2^{-2} = 2^{-\frac{t}{T}}} \)
\( \pmb{t = 2T} \)
