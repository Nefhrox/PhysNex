Text of a problem: Body volume \( \pmb{V = 0.002 m³, mass \text{ } m = 3 kg} \) immersed in water. Calculate force \( \pmb{F_A \text{ }} \) \( \pmb{(\rho_w = {1000 kg \over m^3}, \text{ } g=10 \frac{m}{s^2} ). } \)

Answer: \( \pmb{F_A = 20 N} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{F_A = \rho_w V g} \)
