Text of a problem: A diffraction grating of length 1.5cm, contains 3000 lines, the wavelength of light is 550 nm, find grating spacing and maximum diffraction order \( \pmb{m_{max} } \)

Answer:
\( \pmb{d = 5 \cdot 10^{-6}} \)
\( \pmb{m_{max} \approx 9} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{(1) \text{ } d = \frac{l}{N}} \)
\( \pmb{(2) \text{ } m_{max} = \frac{d}{\lambda}} \)
