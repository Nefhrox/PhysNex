Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 3/10

Text of a problem: Young's double slit d = 0.002 m, L = 2 m, \( \pmb{\lambda \text{ = 550nm. Calculate fringe spacing } \Delta x.} \)

Answer:
\( \pmb{\Delta x = 5 \cdot 10^{-4} \text{ m}} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{\Delta x = \frac{\lambda L}{d}} \)
