Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 2/10

Text of a problem: A point charge \(q = 4 \times 10^{-6} \text { } C \). Calculate the potential V at distance r = 0.3 m. ( \(k = 9 \times 10^{9} {Nm^{2} \over C^{2}} \))

Answer: V = 120 kV

Solution: \( V = {k q \over r} \)
