Text of a problem: A conductor with current \( \pmb{I = 6 A \text { and length } l = 0.3 m \text{ in field } B = 0.4 T} \) (angle 90°). Calculate the force F.

Answer: \( \pmb{F = 0.72 N} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{F = B I l sin(90°)} \)
