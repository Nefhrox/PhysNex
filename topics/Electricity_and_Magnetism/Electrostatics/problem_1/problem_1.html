Text of a problem: Two charges \( \pmb{q₁ = q₂ = 5 \times 10^{-6} \text { } C}\) are 0.2 m apart. Calculate the force F ( \( \pmb { k = 9 \times 10^{9} {Nm^{2} \over C^{2}} }\) )

Answer: \( \pmb{F = 5.625 N} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{F = {k q_1 q_2 \over r^{2} } } \)
