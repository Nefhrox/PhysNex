Molecular Physics and Thermodynamics Formulae

Kinetic theory

\( \pmb{p = \frac{1}{3} n m_0 \overline{v^2}} \) - Pressure of an ideal gas

\( \pmb{n = \frac{N}{V}} \) - Number density of molecules

\( \pmb{N = n N_A} \) - Total number of molecules

\( \pmb{M_r = \frac{m}{n}} \) - Molar mass

\( \pmb{\overline{E_k} = \sqrt{\frac{3kT}{m_0}}} \) - Mean square velocity of molecules

\( \pmb{\overline{E_k} = \frac{3}{2} kT} \) - Mean kinetic energy of a molecule

Gas laws

\( \pmb{pV = nRT} \) - Ideal gas law

\( \pmb{\frac{p_1 V_1}{T_1} = \frac{p_2 V_2}{T_2}} \) - Combined gas law

Thermodynamics

\( \pmb{Q = cm\Delta T} \) - Heat

\( \pmb{Q = Lm} \) - Latent heat of vaporization

\( \pmb{Q = \Delta U + A} \) - First law of thermodynamics

\( \pmb{U = \frac{3}{2} nRT} \) - Internal energy of an ideal gas

\( \pmb{A = p\Delta V} \) - Work done by a gas

Heat engines

\( \pmb{\eta = \frac{Q_1-Q_2}{Q_1}} \) - Efficiency of a heat engine

\( \pmb{\eta_{max} = \frac{T_1-T_2}{T_1}} \) - Carnot cycle efficiency

Humidity

\( \pmb{\phi = \frac{p}{p_{sat}} \cdot 100\%} \) - Relative humidity
