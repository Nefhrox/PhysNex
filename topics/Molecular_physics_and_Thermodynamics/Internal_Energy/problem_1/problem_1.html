Text of a problem: Ideal gas in isothermal process. What is \( \pmb{\Delta U} \)?

Answer: \( \pmb{\Delta U = 0} \)

Solution: U depends only on temperature T, \( \text { } \pmb{\Delta T = 0} \Rightarrow \Delta U = 0 \)
