Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 2/10

Text of a problem: A parallel-plate capacitor has plate area S = 200 cm² and separation d = 2 mm. Calculate the capacitance C in vacuum. ( \( \pmb{ε₀ = 8.85 \times 10^{-12} {F \over m}} \) )

Answer: \( \pmb{C \approx 8.85 \times 10^{-11} F} \)

Solution: \( \pmb{C = {ε₀ S \over d}} \)
