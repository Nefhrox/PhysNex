Type: calculation

Text of a problem: Two bodies with masses of 2 kilograms and 3 kilograms are located at a distance of 1 meter from each other. Find the gravitational force between them. The gravitational constant ( \( \pmb{G = 6.67 \cdot 10^{-11} \text{)}} \)

Answer:
\( \pmb{F = 4 \cdot 10^{-10} N} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}} \)
