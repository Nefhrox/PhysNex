\( \)                              - start and end of a place where you should write all what you use from MathJax

\text{}                            - regular text 

\pmb{}                             - font

\frac{numerator}{denumerator}      - fraction

"numerator" \over "denumenator"    - same as frac  

\times                             - multiplication (cross sign)

\cdot                              - multiplication (dot sign)

^{}                                - to the power of  

_{}                                - subscripts

\sqrt{}                            - square root of

\approx                            - approximately equal

\pi, \lamda, \rho...               - greek letters

\Rightarrow                        - therefore

\propto                            - directly proportional

\Delta                             - delta sign

\overset{}{}                       - put one sign on another
