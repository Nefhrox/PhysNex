Text of a problem: Ideal gas \( \pmb{\nu =\text{2 mol, } T = 400 \text{ K, V = 0,1} m^{3}} \). Calculate p ( \( \pmb{R = 8.31 \frac{J}{molK}} \)).

Answer: \( \pmb{p \approx 6.65 \times 10^{5} Pa} \)

Solution:
\( \text{(1)} \pmb{pV = \nu R T}\)
\( \pmb{\text{(2)} p = {\nu R T \over V}} \)
