Problem 2

Type: theory

Difficulty: 5/10

Text of a problem: \( \text{A ball of mass m is tied to a string and moves on a horizontal circle of radius R with constant speed v on a horizontal table.}\)

1: Explain carefully why the tension force does zero work during a full revolution

2: How much work does it do in half revolution

Answer:
\( \pmb{W = 0} \)
\( \pmb{W_{1/2} = 0} \)

Solution:
\(\text{The tension force does no work, since it is always perpendicular to the direction of motion (velocity vector) of the ball} \)
