Type: calculation

Difficulty: 2/10

Text of a problem: A mass of 1 kilogram is attached to a spring with a spring constant of 400 newtons per meter. Find the period of oscillation. \( \pmb{} \)

Answer:
\( \pmb{T \approx 0.314 s} \)

Solution:
\(\text{} \pmb{T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}} \)
