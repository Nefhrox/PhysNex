Problem 1

Type: calculation

Difficulty: 1/10

Text of a problem: A car is moving at a speed of 60 km/h. Its momentum is \( \pmb{1.5 \times 10^{4} \frac{kgm}{s}} \). What is the mass of the car?

Answer: \( \pmb{m = 90 kg} \)

Solution:
\( \pmb{(1) \text { } p = mv} \)
\( \pmb{(2) \text { } m = {p \over v}} \)
